Instructions

To start the game, Ms Choo will be bringing you to a secret location.You have to keep your eye blinfolded. NO CHEATING. After you have arrived, you may search for your first clue. Once you have identified the passcode, you may enter the passcode into the blanks. If the passcode is correct, you will be given a coordinate of your next location. This goes on until the last location.You have unlimited tries for the passcode. HAPPY PLAYING! Good luck ! Have a blast!
